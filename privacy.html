Privacy Policy for Hinduism GPT

This document outlines the privacy policy for Hinduism GPT, a Discord bot developed by the admins of the Hinduism server.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We will only collect and use your personal information in ways that are consistent with this privacy policy.

What Personal Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information about you. This means that we do not collect your Discord username, your Discord ID, your IP address, or the messages you send to Hinduism GPT.

We do not collect any personal information because we do not need it to provide you with the best possible experience using Hinduism GPT. We also do not collect any personal information because we believe that your privacy is important.

We may collect some non-personal information about you, such as the type of device you are using, your operating system, and your browser type. This information is collected anonymously and is used to improve the performance of Hinduism GPT.

How We Use Your Personal Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use any personal information.

How We Protect Your Personal Information

We take your privacy seriously. We use appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

We store all of your data on secure servers that are protected by firewalls and other security measures. We also use encryption to protect your data when it is transmitted over the internet.

Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal information that we collect. You also have the right to request that we delete your personal information.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hinduism server. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be posted on this page.

Contact Us
